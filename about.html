London Gardening Guys

Established in Fulham by two close friends who both discovered they had a phobia for working in an office. The London Garden Guys are saving gardens everyday, and keeping them immaculate every week, all year round. We never say no to a job, we always say yes to a challenge. So if you have recently mistaken your garden for a section of the Amazon rainforest. Or if your BBQ has not been seen since the day you built it. Then The London Garden Guys & Gals will create your outdoor escape.

Landscaping gardens to creating our signature lavender window boxes. And all mowing, weeding and cutting in between. Our network of gardeners, tradesmen and designers ensure your garden, ends up exactly as you want it.

Our cheery team of Garden Guys ensuring the journey to a beautiful garden is as refreshing as the garden your left with.
